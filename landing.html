QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked

What is KOL commerce financing?

Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs) in China drive massive product launches through livestream commerce. Brands need working capital to produce inventory before launch. Tael provides that capital through structured deals, earning yield from the revenue generated by successful launches.

How is principal protected?

Each deal is backed by inventory pledge (physical goods as collateral) and brand receivable assignment (contractual claim on revenue). The yield waterfall prioritizes principal return before any profit distribution.

What are PT and YT tokens?

When you deposit, you receive two tokens: PT (Principal Token) represents your 1:1 redeemable deposit, and YT (Yield Token) represents your rights to the deal's yield. You can hold both to maturity or trade YT separately.

What currencies are accepted?

The protocol accepts USDT and USDC. Our OTC Desk network handles fiat conversion to CNY for brand disbursement and back to stablecoin for your returns.

Is Tael open to everyone?

Currently, Tael operates on an invitation-only basis for qualified allocators. This ensures appropriate risk understanding and maintains deal quality. Request access through our application process.

What happens if a deal underperforms?

The structured waterfall protects principal first. In the rare case of underperformance, yield distribution is reduced proportionally while principal redemption remains prioritized through the collateral structure.
