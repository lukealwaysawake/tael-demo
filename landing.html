TAEL is an invite-only platform that offers structured yield from curated public project financing deals. Each deal is vetted, collateral-backed, and available exclusively to approved participants.

PT (Principal Token) represents your original deposit, redeemable 1:1 at maturity. YT (Yield Token) represents the right to receive yield from the underlying commerce deal.

Each deal is structured with project collateral, verified receivables, and performance covenants. All risk parameters and terms are transparently disclosed before allocation.

PT holders redeem their principal in full. YT holders receive accumulated yield. The deal settles based on actual campaign performance.
