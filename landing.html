TAEL is a structured yield protocol that underwrites influencer commerce campaigns and tokenizes them into fixed-duration yield products. Depositors earn transparent, asset-backed returns.

PT (Principal Token) represents your original deposit, redeemable 1:1 at maturity. YT (Yield Token) represents the right to receive yield from the underlying commerce deal and can be traded.

Each deal is structured with inventory collateral, platform receivables, and KOL performance guarantees. Risk parameters are transparently disclosed before allocation.

At maturity, PT holders redeem their principal in full. YT holders receive their accumulated yield. The deal settles based on the actual commerce campaign performance.
